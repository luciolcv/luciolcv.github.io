Biography

I am a PhD Student in Information and Communication Technologies at the Department of Computer Engineering, Modeling, Electronics, and Systems Engineering (DIMES) of the University of Calabria, Italy.

Currently, I am teaching assistant for the Big Data Analytics and Data Mining courses of the Master Degree in Computer Engineering.

My main research interests include social networks and media analysis, with a particular focus on Decentralized Online Social Networks (DOSNs).
